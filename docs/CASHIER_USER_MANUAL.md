# Cashier User Manual — Aydın GROS OS V1.0

Welcome to the Aydın GROS cash register operator system. Follow these instructions to handle daily store checkouts.

---

## 1. Starting a Shift
1. Launch the cash register screen.
2. Enter your email and cashier PIN code.
3. On the **Kasa Açılış** dialog, enter the opening cash drawer amount (default: `500.00 TL`) and click **Kasayı Aç**.

---

## 2. Scanning & Weighing Items

### Barcoded Products
1. Hold the product barcode in front of the scanner.
2. The item will automatically appear in the basket list.

### Weighted/Bulk Products (e.g. Fruits, Vegetables)
1. Place the product on the connected weighing scale.
2. Type the product name or shortcode (PLU) on the search grid (e.g. "Domates").
3. Click the product button. The scale will weigh the product automatically and update the basket with the correct price.

---

## 3. Handling Payments

### Single Payment
- **Nakit (Cash):** Click the green **Nakit** button. Enter the amount paid by the customer to calculate the change.
- **Kredi Kartı (Card):** Click the **Kredi Kartı** button to authorize transaction via the Yeni Nesil ÖKC.

### Split Payment (Karma Ödeme)
1. Click the **Karma Ödeme** button on the bottom panel.
2. On the popup, enter the target cash amount (e.g. `20 TL`) and card amount (e.g. `9 TL`).
3. Click **Ödemeyi Tamamla** to print the invoice check.

---

## 4. Voids & Manager Lockouts
- **Voiding a Single Item:** Click the red trash icon next to the item.
- **Clearing the Basket:** Click **Sepeti Temizle**.
- *Note: If scan speed exceeds the normal cashier threshold or if weight mismatches are detected, the screen will lock automatically. Call the store manager to swipe their authorization card.*
