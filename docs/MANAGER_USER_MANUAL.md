# Manager User Manual — Aydın GROS OS V1.0

This guide is for store managers and supervisors. It defines backoffice workflows, pricing catalog approvals, register overrides, and end-of-day closures.

---

## 1. Supervisor Overrides (Cashier Lockouts)
If a cashier triggers a fraud lock (scan velocity anomalies, bulk weight mismatch, or manual basket voiding of values > 50 TL):
1. The POS register screen will lock, displaying: **"Yetkili Onayı Gerekiyor"**.
2. Enter your manager email and password, or scan your supervisor card.
3. Click **Onayla** to unlock the screen.

---

## 2. End of Day: Z-Reports & Shift Closure
A Z-Report closes the active register database session and commits all transaction numbers to compliance records.
1. Click **Kasa İşlemleri** on the POS sidebar.
2. Select **Z-Raporu Al ve Kapat**.
3. Confirm the action.
4. The cash drawer will open automatically. Perform cash count validation.
5. Review the printed slip summary (total cash sales, total card sales, VAT breakdowns).

---

## 3. Dynamic Price Book Approvals
To activate promotional or regional branch pricing:
1. Navigate to the **Pricing Book Portal** on the backoffice dashboard.
2. Select the target price book (e.g. "Sakarya Bölge Özel").
3. Review entries and click **Approve** to deploy prices across target checkouts.
